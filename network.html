The Network

Meet the Team

ERNEST is powered by a diverse European network of institutions, researchers, educators, and creative professionals working together to transform science into immersive experiences. At the heart of the project is a multidisciplinary consortium that combines scientific excellence with expertise in education, communication, and game design. From physicists and astrophysicists to teachers, facilitators, and designers, each member contributes to building engaging escape room experiences grounded in real research
