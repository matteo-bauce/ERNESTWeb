What the project does

Science communication built as an adventure.

From concepts to experiences

ERNEST develops escape rooms set in the worlds of physics and astronomy, where participants solve clues inspired by scientific methods, space exploration, light, matter, and the universe.

Each activity is designed to make research approachable, hands-on, and memorable, giving audiences a playful way to engage with scientific thinking.

Who it is for

The project welcomes teenagers, adults, families, schools, and curious visitors who want to encounter science in a lively and collaborative format.

Where it happens

ERNEST escape rooms can be installed across different contexts, with a special role during the European Researchers' Night and other public science engagement events.
